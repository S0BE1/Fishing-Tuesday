Fishing Tuesday: The Inception

The concept was born, one spring afternoon- the time clock was punched, so the race was on. To gather the beer and the gear- but which lake to fish on? This day, which happened to be a Tuesday, would continue on to become a great tradition. One that has stood the test of time. (Rain or shine)
